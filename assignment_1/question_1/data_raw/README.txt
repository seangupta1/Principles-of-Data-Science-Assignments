Dataset: Frailty

File Format: CSV (UTF-8 encoding)

Columns: 5
Rows: 10

Columns:
- Height: (double) height in inches
- Weight: (integer) weight in lbs
- Age: (integer) age in years
- Grip strength: (integer) in kilograms measured with dynamometer
- Frailty: (categorical): Y = symptoms present, N = symptoms absent

Notes:
- No missing values were present in the dataset
