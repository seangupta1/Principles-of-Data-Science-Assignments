Dataset: Diabetes

File Format: CSV (UTF-8 encoding)

Columns: 9
Rows: 768

Columns:
- Pregnancies: (integer) number of times the patient has been pregnant
- Glucose: (integer) blood glucose concentration
- BloodPressure: (integer) blood pressure
- SkinThickness: (integer) skin thickness
- Insulin: (integer) insulin level
- BMI: (float) body mass index
- DiabetesPedigreeFunction: (float) likelihood of diabetes based on patients history
- Age: (integer) age of patient
- Outcome: (integer binary categorical) diabetes diagnosis (1 = diabetic, 0 = non-diabetic)

Notes:
- Some features are entered as zero if missing
